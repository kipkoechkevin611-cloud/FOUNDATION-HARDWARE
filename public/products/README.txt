HOW TO ADD YOUR OWN PRODUCT PHOTOS
====================================

1. Take a photo of each product with your phone camera
2. Copy the photo into THIS folder (public/products/)
3. Name it exactly like the product ID, e.g.:
   - cement.jpg
   - corrugated-30g.jpg
   - tank-1000l.jpg
   - barbed-wire-ngombe-480m.jpg
   - solar-panel-350w.jpg
   (see lib/data.ts for all product IDs)

4. Then update the image in lib/data.ts from:
   image: "https://images.pexels.com/..."
   to:
   image: "/products/cement.jpg"

That's it! Your own photos will show on the website.

TIP: Good photo tips:
- Photograph on a clean floor or white background
- Make sure lighting is good (near a window)
- Take portrait/square photo if possible
- Even phone photos work great!
