HOW TO ADD RANDTECH PRODUCTS TO YOUR WEBSITE
=============================================

STEP 1: Visit https://www.randtech.co.ke/ and browse their products

STEP 2: For each product you want to add, note down:
  - Product Name
  - Randtech Price
  - Category (Building Materials, Electrical, Plumbing, etc.)

STEP 3: Calculate YOUR price (50% of Randtech's price):
  YOUR PRICE = Randtech Price × 0.5
  
  Example: If Randtech sells cement at KES 700, your price = 700 × 0.5 = KES 350

STEP 4: Open lib/data.ts and copy this template for each product:

{
  id: "unique-id",
  name: "Product Name",
  category: "Category Name",
  description: "Brief description (write your own, don't copy)",
  image: "https://images.unsplash.com/photo-XXXXXXXX?w=400&auto=format&fit=crop&q=60",
  price: YOUR_CALCULATED_PRICE,
  unit: "piece/bag/meter/etc",
  specifications: ["Spec 1", "Spec 2"],
  inStock: true
},

EXAMPLE - Adding Randtech's cement product:
Randtech Price: KES 700 (Bamburi Cement 50kg)
Your Price: 700 × 0.5 = KES 350

Template entry:
{
  id: "bamburi-cement",
  name: "Bamburi Cement 50kg",
  category: "Building Materials",
  description: "High quality Bamburi Portland cement for construction projects",
  image: "https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1513467535987-fd81bc7d62f8?w=400&auto=format&fit=crop&q=60",
  price: 350,
  unit: "bag",
  specifications: ["50kg bag", "Grade 32.5R", "Bamburi brand"],
  inStock: true
},

IMAGE SOURCES (Free, Legal):
- https://unsplash.com (search for: cement, steel, pipes, solar panels, etc.)
- https://pexels.com
- https://pixabay.com

HOW TO FIND IMAGES:
1. Go to unsplash.com
2. Search for your product type (e.g., "cement bags")
3. Click an image
4. Click "Download free"
5. Copy the image URL or download and upload to your server
